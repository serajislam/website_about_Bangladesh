Bangladesh a a glance




Official Name :   The People republic of Bangladesh
Capital :   Dhaka
Language :   Bangla
Nationality :   Bangladeshi
Currency :   Taka(TK) Denominations: Notes:Taka 1000, 500, 100, 50, 20, 10, 5, 2 & 1 Coins: Taka 5, 2, 1 and Paisa 50, 25, 10, 5, 2 & 1
Independence Day :   March 26
Victory day :   Dec 16
Prime Minister :   Sheikh Hasina(2009-Present)
President :   Abdul Hamid(2013-Present)
Speaker of the house :   Dr. Shirin Sharmin Chaudhury (2013-Present)
Education Board :   Eleven
Division :   Eight
District :   Sixty Four
Upazila :   Four Hundred Ninty two (2017)
Largest District :   Rangamati
Smallest District :   Meherpur
Largest Division :   Chittagong
Smallest Division :   Barisal
Largest City :   Dhaka
Area :   56977 sq. miles or 147570 sq. km.
Religion :   Islam but other main religions namely Hinduism, Buddhism, Christianity are practiced in peace and harmony.
National Poet :   Kazi Nazrul Islam
National Dress :   Sari (female) and Kurta (male)
National Calendar :   Bonggabdo
National River :   Jomuna
National Game :   Kabadi (Ha-Du-Du)
National Park :   Bhawal National Park
National Mosque :   Baitul Mukarrom
National Temple :   Dhakeshori Temple
National Museum :   National Museum of Bangladesh
National Library :   National Library of Bangladesh
Population :   163 Million(2016)
Boundary :   North: India, West: India, South: Bay of Bengal, East: India and Myanmar
Territorial Water :   200 nautical miles.
Time Zone :   BST (UTC+6)
Date format :   dd-mm-yyyy
Total GDP :   $285.817 billion(2018)
Per Capita :   $1,754
Calling Code :   +880
Geographycal Location :   Between 20°34′ and 26°38′ north latitude and between 88°01′ and 92°41′ east longitude.
National Anthem :   The first ten lines of "Amar Sonar Bangla". written by Nobel Laureate Rabindranath Tagore.
National Flag :   Consists of a circle coloured red throughout its area, resting on a green rectangular background. The length to width ratio of the rectangle is 10:6 and the circle has a radius of one fifth of the length.
